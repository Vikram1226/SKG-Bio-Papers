SKG Bio Papers

SKG Bio Papers is an authorised dealer of MEND-TECH, a CPCB certified company for compostable products. We focus on replacing conventional plastic with responsible alternatives.

The ideal replacement for conventional plastic is a compostable bag produced from cornstarch and plant-based materials that align with IS/ISO 17088 standards.

These bags can be used by shops, retailers, showrooms, industries, hotels, and homes, making sustainable daily tasks practical and scalable.
